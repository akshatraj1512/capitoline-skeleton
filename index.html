Capitoline Global Group is a proprietary trading firm operating in complex, data-driven markets. The desk trades its own capital across power and digital asset markets, combining structured research, disciplined execution, and rigorous risk controls.

Rather than relying on third-party systems, Capitoline develops its own analytical platforms to support live decision-making. This ensures alignment between strategy, execution, and risk.
